About MANPOWER

The Ultimate Sports Management System for P P Savani University

🎯 Our Mission

MANPOWER is a comprehensive, state-of-the-art Sports Management System designed exclusively for P P Savani University (PPSU), Kosamba, Surat, Gujarat. Our platform empowers students to discover, enroll in, and track their sports activities seamlessly. We bridge the gap between students, coaches and administrators — making sports participation organized, accessible and engaging for everyone on campus.

🚀 What We Offer

🎮

30+ Sports

From Football and Cricket to Chess and Yogasan – browse and enroll in over 30 sports categories offered at PPSU.

🏟️

Live Tournaments

Admins launch tournaments with real-time notifications sent to all students. RSVP and compete for glory!

📊

Performance Tracking

Track your enrolled games, activity history, achievement badges, and standings on the leaderboard.

📅

Schedules & Events

Stay updated with upcoming practice sessions, tournaments, and campus sports events — all in one place.

🔔

Instant Notifications

Receive alerts for new tournaments, schedule changes, and important announcements in real-time.

👤

Role-Based Access

Students, Coaches, and Admins each have tailored dashboards with features specific to their role.

🏫 About P P Savani University

P P Savani University is a premier private university located at NH-8, Village Dhamdod, Kosamba, Dist. Surat, Gujarat – 394125, India. Established with a vision to provide world-class education, PPSU is recognized for its modern infrastructure, innovative teaching, and a strong focus on holistic student development. The Sports Department at PPSU is committed to nurturing athletic talent, promoting physical fitness, and building a competitive sports culture across all disciplines.
